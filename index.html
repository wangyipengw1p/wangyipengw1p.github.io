Hi! Thank you for stopping by my site!

I am a fourth-year Bachelor student in Electronic Information Engineering in Beihang University. My major is IC and system design.

During the past 3 years, I had the privilege of working with Prof. Wei S. Zhao (@Beihang U), Dr. Liang Liu (@Lund U) and Prof. Marian Verhelst (@KU Leuven). Currently, I'm doing bachelor thesis with Prof. Yongpan Liu (@ Tsinghua U & Pi2star Tech).

My research interests includes Digital IC design, Software-hardware codesign, VLSI architecture for media info processing and machine learning.

Phase noise(PN) compensation using dedicated pilot for 5G with ASIC
Minkeun Chuang, Yipeng Wang, Liang Liu
Area: Massive MIMO, wireless communication, Digital design

We Implemented a hardware friendly algorithm for PN compensation using the dedicated pilot in 5G-OFDM. Through loosing the feedback loop and adding parallelism we achieved chieved the following implementation result: at least 500MHz for both 65nm balk and 28 nm ST FDSOI and at most 8 parallel; throughput reached at least 4 G complex num/s.
